About Us

Priti Charoskar
Founder & Owner

Welcome to Spice Bite – a celebration of India’s rich culinary heritage. Located in the heart of the city, our restaurant brings you an authentic taste of traditional Indian recipes passed down through generations.

From fragrant biryanis to sizzling tandoori delicacies, every dish is crafted with the finest ingredients and aromatic spices that capture the essence of Indian culture. Whether you're here for a hearty North Indian curry or a tangy South Indian delight, there's something for every palate.

At Spice Bite, we believe in more than just food – we offer an experience. Our warm ambiance, inspired by Indian artistry and hospitality, makes every meal memorable.
